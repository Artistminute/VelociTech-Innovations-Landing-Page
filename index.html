Hey there, I'm Keith!

I've got a passion for 3D printing that's taken me on quite the journey. After spending four years in the US Navy, I was introduced to the world of 3D printing during my time off. Using Solidworks, I found myself fascinated with the creative possibilities and technical intricacies of the field. When I left the Navy, I knew that I wanted to continue pursuing my newfound passion. That's why I decided to start up my very own 3D print farm here in Palm Coast - a place where people could come to get their parts printed in mass quantities. With my knowledge of Solidworks and laser scanning, I was confident that I could offer a service that would make a real difference. And so here I am, running a thriving 3D printing business that's helped countless people bring their ideas to life. I take great pride in what I do, and I'm always excited to see what new challenges my customers will bring my way. So whether you're a seasoned 3D printing pro or just starting out, I'd love to help you achieve your goals. Let's get printing!

Services

3D Printing with the Bambu X-1 Carbon

I take great pride in offering top-notch 3D printing services using cutting-edge technology. At the heart of my operation is the Bambu Lab X-1 Carbon printer, which is capable of printing using a wide variety of materials including PAHT, PET, PLA, ABS, PC and PA. This means that no matter what your project requires, I can deliver high-quality results that meet your specifications.

Laser Scanning HP3D Structured Light Scanner Pro S3

In addition to 3D printing, I also offer 3D scanning services using the HP 3D Structured Light Scanner Pro S3. This powerful scanner allows me to quickly and accurately capture detailed scans of real-world objects, which can then be used as a basis for 3D models or other applications. Whether you're looking to reverse engineer an existing part, create a digital archive of a historical artifact, or anything in between, my scanning services can help bring your vision to life.
